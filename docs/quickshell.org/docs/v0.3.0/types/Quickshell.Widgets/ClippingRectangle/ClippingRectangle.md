# ClippingRectangle
**Version:** v0.3.0
**Description:** Rectangle capable of clipping content inside its border.

## Properties
- `clipRadius`: `real` - The radius of the clipping region.
- `clipRadiusTopLeft`: `real` - The top left radius of the clipping region.
- `clipRadiusTopRight`: `real` - The top right radius of the clipping region.
- `clipRadiusBottomLeft`: `real` - The bottom left radius of the clipping region.
- `clipRadiusBottomRight`: `real` - The bottom right radius of the clipping region.
